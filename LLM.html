Text The Universal Interface

Visit Website

Pen and Paper is too oldschool and you don't want to stand up? Let's do that online! You have no friends to play with? No Problem! You play against AI. I wanted create a quick bossfight against a beast of any kind. In it the Player has to choose between fighting, defending or magic wich can only be used once per game.

The basic trainig of the ValTown LLM and outlining the rules for the game went really well. Likewise was the building of a theme fitting website where I experimented with backgrounds and button images.

But the problems started early with cosmetic changes of the LLMs Json Output. No matter what I wrote, how often I asked another AI and Fabian or threatend to harm small kitties, the output always looked the same. Frustrating. It was definetly not my topic as I was loosing myself in prompting the outlines of the game when the tast was to be creative and not build a chatbot. In spite of all I still learned a lot from the tast. How clear you have to state promts to the LLM or to mind the fact that too much text can 'confuse' the LLM.
