Code Of Nature

Visit Website

Searching through Amrum on an excursion to find an interesting movement in untouched nature a few things came to mind. The sea waves and their remains in the sand, the flocks of nesting birds. But what caught my eye in the end was a little bush that grew all over the island. The way it split up into new branches kind of interested me and gave me ideas how I could translate into code.

I achieved progress through experimenting with Fabians coded shrub wich was kinda already how I imagined the bush to look like. So from there on out I was only playing around. But decided to convert another bush i found into code. This one didn't grew upwards but rather up and falling down again.

Sadly because of the fact that I couldn't code that well that the branching and falling looked lifelike and I was too scared to ask anyone I also let that idea go. I then started to zoom into the shrub and chose to randomize a stick. Every click shows a new twig. In the end I am quite satisfies with the website. The end result not that much as I could've gotten more out of the topic but that aside I think I learned a lot from it. Not only about coding and trying to translate real life movements but also spotting them and actually beeing more aware.
